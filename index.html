A little bit about me

Originally from Ukraine, now happily settled in Ontario. I've brought a lot of that journey into how I work — adaptable, detail-oriented, and not easily put off by a challenge

I think in layouts

Design is usually where I start — figuring out the structure, the flow, the feel. I care about things being intuitive and visually considered, not just technically correct.

I build what I design

I'm comfortable on both sides of the handoff — as likely to be adjusting a layout as I am writing the code that brings it to life. Design and development feel like one thing to me, not two.

I'm always learning

I'm on a path toward full-stack development and genuinely enjoy the process of picking things up. You can catch some of that progress over on my GitHub.

Design beyond screens

My work isn't limited to the web. I've worked on branding, print, and digital design projects across a range of formats — from pitch decks to mobile apps to social media.

I find it hard to separate good design from good code — when both are done thoughtfully, they just feel like one thing. Tetiana Ronska
